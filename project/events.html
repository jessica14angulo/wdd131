Latest Events

Learn AI with Python

PyLadies Cochabamba

March 15, 2025

Learn AI with Python is an event organized by PyLadies that introduces participants to the fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence using the Python programming language. Designed to be inclusive and beginner-friendly, the event combines theory with hands-on coding sessions, allowing attendees to explore key AI concepts such as machine learning, data processing, and model training. Through live workshops, collaborative projects, and mentorship, participants gain practical skills and confidence to start their journey in AI. Hosted by local PyLadies chapters like PyLadies Cochabamba, the event also fosters a supportive community that encourages more women and underrepresented groups to thrive in the world of tech and AI.

Google I/O

Google Developer Groups

November 30, 2024

Google I/O is an annual developer conference organized by Google and supported by Google Developer Groups (GDGs) around the world. It brings together developers, tech enthusiasts, and industry leaders to explore the latest innovations in Google's technology ecosystem. The event features keynotes, technical sessions, hands-on workshops, and product announcements related to Android, Chrome, Google Cloud, AI, machine learning, and more. GDG-hosted I/O Extended events offer local communities the chance to experience Google I/O through livestreams, discussions, and interactive activities, making cutting-edge knowledge and networking opportunities accessible to developers everywhere.

Upcoming Events

Woman Game Jam Hackathon

Women Techmakers

August 8-10, 2025

Women Game Jam is an annual, non-competitive game development marathon dedicated to empowering cisgender, transgender, and non-binary women within the gaming industry. Participants are challenged to plan and create digital or analog games based on a theme revealed at the start of the event. The jam fosters a supportive environment that encourages learning, portfolio building, and the exchange of experiences between beginners and experts. By strengthening local female development communities, Women Game Jam aims to increase diversity and inclusion in game development. The upcoming edition is scheduled for August 8-10, 2025.
